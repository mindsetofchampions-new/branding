Philadelphia-centered, youth-powered. We build lasting legacy in our neighborhoods.

Voice & Tone

Bold & Confident

Use strong, declarative statements. Lead with impact. Avoid hedging language.

"We're building champions on and off the game floor."
Inspiring & Empowering

Speak to potential and possibility. Celebrate achievements. Encourage action.

"Esports isn't just a game - it's a gateway."
Youth-Centered

Energetic but not childish. Respectful of young people's intelligence. Culturally aware and relevant.

Professional & Credible

Back claims with evidence. Use proper terminology. Maintain organizational authority without being distant.

✓ Do
  • → Use active voice
  • → Keep sentences clear and direct
  • → Include calls to action
  • → Speak to transformation and impact
✗ Don't
  • → Use jargon without explanation
  • → Be condescending toward youth
  • → Overpromise or exaggerate
  • → Use passive, weak language

06 / Framework

C.H.A.M.P.I.O.N.S.

Nine pillars of development organized into three phases of growth. Each pillar has a dedicated color, icon, and visual identity.

01
Foundation
Who I am + How I show up
C
Character
Foundation

Development of core values, integrity, and personal character as the bedrock of everything we do.

H
Habits
Foundation

Building daily practices and routines that create momentum, consistency, and lasting positive change.

A
Appearance
Foundation

Presenting yourself with professionalism and intention. First impressions open doors; character keeps them open.

02
Direction
Why I do it + How I think
M
Mission
Direction

Defining your purpose and aligning your actions to a clear, compelling reason for everything you pursue.

P
Positive Attitude
Direction

Cultivating an optimistic, growth-oriented mindset that transforms challenges into opportunities.

I
Intentionality
Direction

Acting with deliberate purpose. Every decision, relationship, and action is made with awareness and intent.

03
Launch
How I navigate the world + Legacy
O
Obstacles
Launch

Developing resilience to identify, face, and overcome barriers that stand between you and your goals.

N
Network
Launch

Building authentic relationships and a community of support that multiplies your reach and impact.

S
Success
Launch

Defining and achieving success on your own terms; then paying it forward to uplift the next generation.
