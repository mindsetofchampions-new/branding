# Mindset of Champions Brand Guidelines

> Official brand guidelines for consistent representation of the Mindset of Champions identity.

---

## Brand Identity

### About Mindset of Champions

Mindset of Champions (MOC) is a Philadelphia-based nonprofit organization that leverages esports, media production, and live events to deliver social-emotional learning, career readiness, and leadership development to underserved youth.

### Brand Essence

**Tagline**: "A League. A Lab. A Legacy."

This three-part tagline encapsulates our mission:
- **A League**: Competitive esports that teaches teamwork, discipline, and resilience
- **A Lab**: Hands-on learning in media, technology, and career skills
- **A Legacy**: Building lasting impact in our community and participants' futures

### Brand Values
1. **Empowerment** — We believe in the potential of every young person
2. **Excellence** — We pursue the highest standards in everything we do
3. **Community** — We are rooted in Philadelphia and committed to our neighborhoods
4. **Innovation** — We use cutting-edge platforms to reach and teach youth
5. **Integrity** — We model the character we seek to develop

## Logo

### Primary Logo

The Mindset of Champions logo features a shield-inspired mark with the letters "MOC" integrated into a dynamic, forward-leaning design.

**Logo File Locations**:
- `/public/moc-logo.svg` — Primary logo (SVG)

### Logo Usage Rules

**DO:**
- Use the logo on dark backgrounds for optimal visibility
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M"
- Use provided logo files without modification

**DON'T:**
- Stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
- Change logo colors outside approved variants
- Add effects (shadows, glows, outlines) to the logo
- Place the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce legibility
- Recreate the logo — always use official files

### Logo Minimum Size
- **Digital**: 40px height minimum
- **Print**: 0.5 inch (12.7mm) height minimum

### Logo Color Variants

| Variant | Usage |
|---------|-------|
| **Full Color** | Primary use on dark backgrounds |
| **White** | On red or colored backgrounds |
| **Black** | On light backgrounds (rare use) |

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics

1. **Bold & Confident**
   - Use strong, declarative statements
   - Lead with impact
   - Avoid hedging language

2. **Inspiring & Empowering**
   - Speak to potential and possibility
   - Celebrate achievements
   - Encourage action

3. **Youth-Centered**
   - Energetic but not childish
   - Respectful of young people's intelligence
   - Culturally aware and relevant

4. **Professional & Credible**
   - Back claims with evidence
   - Use proper terminology
   - Maintain organizational authority

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences clear and direct
- Include calls to action
- Speak to transformation and impact

**DON'T:**
- Use jargon without explanation
- Be condescending toward youth
- Overpromise or exaggerate
- Use passive, weak language

### Sample Brand Statements

> "We're building champions on and off the game floor."

> "Esports isn't just a game—it's a gateway to career readiness, leadership, and lasting impact."

> "A League. A Lab. A Legacy."

## Photography & Imagery

### Photography Style

- **Energy**: High-energy, action-focused shots
- **Subjects**: Youth engaged in gaming, events, learning, collaboration
- **Authenticity**: Real participants, genuine moments
- **Diversity**: Represent the diversity of Philadelphia's youth

### Treatment

- Dark, moody backgrounds preferred
- Gradient overlays to integrate with brand colors
- Avoid heavy filters that distort reality

### Image Overlays

When placing text over images:
```
Gradient Overlay:
- Top: 80% opacity black
- Middle: 60% opacity black  
- Bottom: 100% opacity black (fade to background)
```

## Digital Applications

### Website

- **Background**: MOC Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Navigation**: Transparent → solid on scroll

- Red CTAs for primary actions
- Consistent header with logo
